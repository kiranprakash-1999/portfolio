Cooking the world's cuisine

My mum is the reason I enjoy cooking, and my culinary inspiration. Since she grew up in Bangalore, our regular food is south Indian, and I feel spoiled. There are so many curries, dishes, snacks, and sweets that I can enjoy I wanted to learn how to make them. My mum taught me a lot when I was a kid, and I grew up to enjoy cooking. Now, I make dishes from all over the world, and have spent lots of time experimenting with new ideas for food.
