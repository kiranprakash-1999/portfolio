Producing music, playing piano, and performing

Having a hobby that was primarily intended to be artistic and soulful was important to me, and music fits the bill. From a young age I enjoyed singing, rapping, and generally performing. I auditioned for Canada's Got Talent (didn't make it past the first round, but it was fun), performed at many talent shows in my schools, and occasionally post covers of my own. All of this was fun but if I was going to invest time into this, I wanted to learn and get good at it. Eventually my affinity toward technology let me to music production which I found fascinating. I have been making songs since, learning how to produce along the way. In no way am I good in a way that I want to share :P but I hope to be one day!

Along my journey of producing, I learned that learning an instrument is probably a pretty good foundation for producing music. Consequetly, I bought a piano and started pushing keys. I am still a beginner but being self taught has its own hardships; but I am diligently grinding my Hanon excercises, and learning a lot. Its been fruitful to see how much I've improve, but I still have a long way to go; the journey will be great.
