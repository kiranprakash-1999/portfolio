About Me

I am a UBC Mechatronics Engineering graduate that really enjoys building automation, robotics, and biomedical technology as a career. I am passionate about all the pillars of mechatronics (mechanical, electrical, software) so I delve deep into each for my builds. I am proficient and firmware design, CAD, circuit and PCB design.


I also enjoy music production and playing the piano, although I am a beginner a both! Having at least one soulful hobby was important for me to have, and music certainly resonated. Moreover, I am developing a VR tutoring class and curricula. I have always found it fulfilling and gratifying to be able to teach and enrich the education of my students.

I am a person of many places. First born and lived in Bangalore, India for 1 year; then, in Newport, Wales for 8 years; then, in Brampton, Ontario for 10 years; then Vancouver for 6 years of study. My family also moved to Nova Scotia in 2020, where I've lived when I return home. Evidently, I am a person who has grown to appreciate the fruits the different cultures and have become an amalgam of them.

For example, I appreciate the particular politeness found in European countries but I welcome the intimacy of interactions of those living in North America. Moreover, I prefer the more relaxed working cultures found in the UK yet I follow the rigorous studying habits from my Indian upbringing. Living in many different cities, attending so many different schools, I've learned to understand that peoples' actions that may seem malevolent or abnormal are just different. They are different because they've lead lives as unique as mine and yours.
